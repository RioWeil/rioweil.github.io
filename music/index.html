A Capella Arrangements

Ditto X Hurt (opb. NewJeans)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: A SSAAB mashup of Ditto and Hurt by NewJeans. I performed this arrangement with Samantha Kim, Maria Siminiuc, Anita Zhang, Nerissa Hsiung, and Jocelyn Chun, which you can find here.
For Lovers (opb. Lamp)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: A SATB arrangement of 恋人へ (For Lovers) by Lamp.
Kataomoi (opb. Aimer)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: A Mezzo-Bass duet of カタオモイ (Kataomoi) by Aimer. I recorded a performance with Lydia Li which you can find here.
Matsuri (opb. Fujii Kaze)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: A SATB arrangement of まつり (Matsuri) by Fujii Kaze, co-arranged with Lisa Li. The English version is based on the cover by Will Stetson. A performance of the arrangement with my (former) cappella group Choral Reef can be found here.
Midnight Plastic Love (opb. Mariya Takeuchi & Miki Matsubara)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: A SATB mash-up of Plastic Love by Mariya Takeuchi and Stay With Me by Miki Matsubara. The English translations are based on the covers ((Plastic Love), (Stay With Me)) by Caitlyn Myers.
Racing Into the Night (opb. YOASOBI)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: A SATB arrangement of 夜に駆ける (Racing Into the Night) by YOASOBI. The arrangement is primarily a transcription of Shimo-Ren’s beautiful A cappella cover with various modifications/adaptations.
Rather Be (opb. Clean Bandit)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: A lower voice arrangement of Rather Be by Clean Bandit. Arranged with a multi-layered single-person cover in mind.
Seasons of Love (opb. Original Broadway Cast of Rent)
  • Files: PDF - Musescore
  • Description: An SATBarB arrangement of Seasons of Love by the Original Broadway Cast of Rent. The arrangement is a transcription/edit of Sinfonia’s A capella cover. A performance of the arrangement by my (former) a cappella group Choral Reef can be found here.
